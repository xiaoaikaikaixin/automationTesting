# Freight Charge Creation Steps

## 1. Freight Info
- Open the URL: http://192.168.72.44/NCTS-SIT/Login.aspx
- Enter 'ICOF-441' in User ID field
- Enter 'icoficof' in Password field
- Click 'Login' button
- Wait for system to complete login
- Verify username appears in top-right corner
- Click 'Freight' menu
- Click 'New' link under Freight Charge menu
- Wait for page to fully load
- Select 'Port to Port' from Shipping Term dropdown
- Enter 'United States of America' in 'Country of Destination' field and press Tab
- Click Port of Discharge dropdown
- Select 'Chicago' option
- Click Freight Indication dropdown
- Select 'Actual Freight Rate' option
- Click Shipping Line popup icon
- Enter 'American President Lines' in Name field
- Click Search button
- Verify 'American President Lines' appears in results
- Click Select button
- Enter '100' in Transit Time field
- Click 'Cargo Nature' dropdown
- Select 'N/A' option
- Click Carrier button
- Select first option from dropdown
- Enter 'General Information testing' in General Information field
- Enter '500' in Freight Rate field

## 2. Submission and Release
- Click 'Save & Submit for Release' button
- Click 'OK' in prompt window
- Wait for page reload
- Verify Freight Charge No appears under 'Freight Charge No' field
- Verify Status shows 'Pending Release'
- Click Release button
- Click OK in prompt window
- Wait for page reload
- Verify Status shows 'Released'
