# Unsold Order Creation Steps

## 1. Login and Navigation
- Open the URL: http://192.168.72.44/NCTS-SIT/Login.aspx
- Enter 'ICOF-441' in User ID field
- Enter 'icoficof' in Password field
- Click 'Login' button
- Wait for system to complete login
- Verify username appears in top-right corner
- Select New Trading Slip from Contract menu
- Wait for the Trading Slip - New record to load

## 2. Order Information
- Select the Special Order Type and click 'Unsold Order' checkbox
- Click 'Division' dropdown list and select 'Fatty Alcohol'
- Enter the sales equal number in Sales Equal Number field
- Click 'Supplier' dropdown list and select 'Musim Mas, PT'
- Verify that 'Factory Location' value equals to 'KIM II'
- Click 'Seller' dropdown list and select 'Inter-Continental Oils & Fats Pte. Ltd.'
- Scroll to the bottom of the page
- Verify that the 'Buyer' dropdown has grey background and is not clickable
- Enter the buyer PO number in Buyer PO No. field
- Click 'Currency' dropdown list and select 'USD'
- Verify that the 'Payment Term' dropdown has grey background and is not clickable
- Click 'Basis' dropdown list and select 'CIP'
- Click 'Traded By' dropdown list and select 'CDG USER'

## 3. Save and Verification
- Click 'Save' button
- Extract value from the 'Trading Slip No.' field and 'Approval Status' field
- Verify that the Trading Slip number is displayed
- Verify that the Approval Status equals to 'Draft'
- Click the 'Shipping' tab
